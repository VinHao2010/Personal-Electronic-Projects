# Soldering prep
> Documents my journey of mastering how to solder!

## Project 1 
> This was a simple project, mainly to practice circuit building and soldering skills.

This is a project where I made a circuit that turns a light on as a button is pressed. I connected 3v3 from a external source to a side of a button, then routed it to a 220 ohms resistor, followed by a red light then finally onto the gnd.
