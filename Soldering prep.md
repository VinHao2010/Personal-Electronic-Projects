# Soldering prep
> Documents my journey of mastering how to solder!

## Project 1 
> This was a simple project, mainly to practice circuit building and soldering skills.

This is a project where I made a circuit that turns a light on as a button is pressed. I connected 3v3 from a external source to a side of a button, then routed it to a 220 ohms resistor, followed by a red light then finally onto the gnd.

- Do everything slower

## Project 2
> The purpose of this project was to learn about transistors

For this project, I first connected a 3v3 external source to a blue LED then, onto a 220 ohms resistor. This connected to the right side of a 2N2222 transistor. Furthermore, I then connected the left side of the transistor to the external gnd source. The special thing about this project, was the ability to open the circuit when even a little bit of force was conducted! For example, the blue LED will light when you connect the middle leg of the transistor to a cable connecting to 3v3!
